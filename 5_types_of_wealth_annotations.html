The 5 Types of Wealth

by Sahil Bloom

Read Aug'25-Nov'25

Designing Your Dream Life

  • I had to define the right game, the one that would actually lead to the life I wanted.

Chapter 2: The Five Types of Wealth

  • Social Wealth is defined by a few deep, meaningful, healthy relationships.
  • Financial Wealth is built upon growing income, managing expenses, and investing the difference in long-term assets that compound meaningfully over time.
  • When you measure the right thing, you take the right actions and create the best outcomes.

Chapter 4: The Life Razor

  • The simplest explanation is the best one. Simple is beautiful.
  • Anything asserted without evidence can also be dismissed without.
  • Strong signal through action, not words.
  • I take care of myself physically and mentally so that I can take care of others.
  • I do one good deed each day (and never tell anyone about it).

Chapter 5: Your True North

  • Systems are the daily actions that create forward progress.
  • High-leverage systems are the daily actions that create amplified, asymmetric forward progress.
  • When they’re on, they deploy their energy in a concentrated, glorious burst.

Time Wealth

  • Prioritize the depth of friendships that should come with age.
  • Avoid bought-status games and focus on playing earned-status games, because the prizes are much more fulfilling.
  • Genius, defined as the range of pursuits where you have excellent competency and high interest or passion.
  • Focus on the highest-leverage opportunities.
  • Be a loud listener. After you ask questions, lean in and show your focus and presence with body language, facial expressions, and sounds.
  • Engineer small wins (they become big wins over time).
  • Choosing the right pursuits is a direct path to a more purpose-filled, fulfilling, productive, and successful life.
  • Mastering the art and science of conversation will pay off professionally but, more important, personally, as it leads to the meaningful connections that provide new texture and richness to life.
